About Us


LuTech is Loyola University Chicago's student organization for technology lovers interested in the interesection of computing and social good. We host events like hackathons and workshops so the Loyola student body and our community can benefit in meaningful ways from student-developed technology projects.

We specialize in hosting student-led hackathons at Loyola - events where software developers and smart creatives come together to produce interesting projects that solve real problems. Our events in the past have focused on Chicago sustainability and local hunger issues. Our events in the future will continue to apply student ideas to real-world issues in interesting ways.
