loyola hacks education

when?

October 1st, 2016 | 9am - 6pm | Cuneo 311 (LSC)

what?

Teams of programmers use their skills to further education. Compete and collaborate with fellow loyolans to: create something that could help children focus, create something that allows teachers better lesson-planning, create something that finds you relevant material for a topic. Create something that can help those less fortunate.

who?

Everyone. Undergraduates, graduates, faculty, and staff. The entire Loyola family is welcome. Some programming experience is recommended, though, anyone who is passionate about education is also welcome to attend. Come as a group or alone
