loyola hacks for health

when?

February 20th, 2016 | 10am - 4pm | TBD

what?

Groups of students will work together to prototype and design an innovation or improvement to an aspect of healthcare using creativity, technology, and teamwork.

who?

Everyone. Undergraduates, graduates, faculty, and staff. The entire Loyola family is welcome. No programming experience is necessary. Our hackathons have technology at their core, but in addition to programmers & engineers, we are looking for participants with all relevant skill sets! If you are interested in health & fitness this event is for you.

Please let us know you are coming here.

Loyola Hacks for Health will look at the health sector through the lens of technology. We will create tangible proposals and prototypes at the hackathon that will be judged by a panel of university faculty. The winner of the hackathon, and all who participate, will be featured on both Loyola & LuTech's websites.

Prizes will be awarded to the most interesting, realistic, and well-designed hackathon entry.
