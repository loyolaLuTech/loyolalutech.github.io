LuTech is proud to put on "Loyola Hacks", an event targeted at pulling IES students and CS enthusiasts together to create software that address sustainability. In this event, we will create projects with a tight time crunch. A fully functioning concept is not expected. Take this time to flesh out a basic prototype, network, and have fun.

Who?
Anyone with a passion for Sustainability or Programming is encouraged to participate.
When?
Saturday, October 17th.
9:30 - 17:00
Where?
Institute for Environmental Sustainability 116, next to San Fco. Hall

In a nutshell. Small teams of programmers will get together with IES students to decide on a workable concept and craft it. LuTech members will be providing mild assistance and food throughout the event. When time is over, the prototypes will be judged by a panel. The winner will have contributed to a significant open source project and be featured on our webiste.
