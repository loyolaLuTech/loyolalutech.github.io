loyola hacks for hunger

when?

November 7, 2015 | 10am - 4pm | Cuneo 210

what?

Groups of students will work together on prototyping and designing a way alleviate or reframe the issue of hunger in our world at any level.

who?

Everyone. Students, grad students, faculty and staff. The whole Loyola Family. All fields of study are welcomed and encourage to join!

Please let us know you are coming here.

Loyola Hacks for Hunger, in conjuction with Loyola Hunger Week 2015, will frame the issue of hunger through the lens of technology. We will come up with tangible proposals/prototypes at the event which will be discussed with the whole group. All projects will be featured online after the event.
